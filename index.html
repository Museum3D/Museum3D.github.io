Digitisation of museum artefacts

A series of workshops focusing on the digitisation of museum artefacts. We focus on the use of high-throughput CT scanning and available opensource software.

Overview

The workshops offer a place for researchs, museum curators and member of the public to come together and and find solutions to the issues they currently face with digitastions and reduce bottlenecks in the system. We kindly thank the Software Sustianability Instuitue for supporting the workshops.
